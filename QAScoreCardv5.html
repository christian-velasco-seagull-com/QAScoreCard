Composite score weights
The composite score is a blend of two groups: the 5 primary metrics (weighted by %) and the severity group (weighted by pts within its group). The severity influence % controls how much the severity group pulls on the final score vs the 5 metrics.

Tier thresholds
Each metric is scored by comparing its value against four named tiers. The three threshold columns are the upper bounds for Excellent, Good, and Moderate — values above Moderate fall into Poor. Scores map linearly: Excellent = 100–75 · Good = 75–50 · Moderate = 50–25 · Poor = 25–0.
Severity rows use defects/SP — the defect count for that severity divided by story points completed. For example, 9 S3 defects in a 247 SP sprint = 0.036 defects/SP.
